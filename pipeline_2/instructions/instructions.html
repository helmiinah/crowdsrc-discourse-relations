This task requires good English skills!

How to perform the task

  1. Read the instructions below and make sure you understand them.
  2. The goal is to produce descriptions that are similar to the correct answers in the training and exam.
  3. Examine the diagram image carefully.
  4. Look at the two outlined diagram elements A and B. Describe the relationship between these two elements concisely. Do not describe the whole diagram, or summarize its contents, only focus on the relationship between the two outlined elements.
  5. Only fill in the text box between A and B. For example, if your description is A describes B, you only need to write describes.
  6. In order for your answer to form a valid phrase, you need to use a verb.
  7. Click Submit to submit the completed tasks.

Payment information: You are paid $0.15 per task suite. You will be paid immediately after the work is completed if your answer is valid English and you attempted to follow the instructions.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a master's thesis research conducted at the University of Helsinki.
