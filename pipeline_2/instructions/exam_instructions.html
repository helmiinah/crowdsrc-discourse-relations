This task requires good English skills!

How to perform the task

  1. Read the instructions below and make sure you understand them.
  2. Examine the diagram image carefully.
  3. Look at the two outlined diagram elements A and B. Choose the option that accurately describes the relationship between the two outlined elements A and B. The goal is not to choose the option that describes the whole diagram.
  4. Click Submit to submit the completed tasks.

To pass the training, 70% of your answers need to be correct.
To pass the examination, 80% of your answers need to be correct.

If you pass the exam, you will get access to tasks were you need to produce descriptions similar to the correct answers in the training and exam.

Payment information: You are paid $0.14 per task suite. You will be paid immediately after the work is completed.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a master's thesis research conducted at the University of Helsinki.
